STORK

About the Shoebill Stork:
A prehistoric looking bird, almost dinosaur-like. They are human-sized birds that are gentle,
but can be dangerous if provoked. They aren't harmful to humans if you bow to them and show respect.

Facts about the Shoebill Stork:
*They make sound by clapping their beak really fast, and it sounds like a machine gun!
*They are around 5ft tall, but only weigh about 16 pounds! And yes, they can fly!
